Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Library Manager ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application and related services, including features that integrate with third-party platforms such as TikTok.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information such as name, email address, and password.
  • Content you upload, including videos, images, captions, and metadata.
  • Settings and preferences you configure within the App.

1.2 Information collected from third-party platforms

When you connect your TikTok account to the App through TikTok's official Login Kit and Content Posting API, we receive only the information you explicitly authorize, which may include:

  • Your TikTok open ID and union ID (anonymous identifiers).
  • Your TikTok display name, username, and avatar URL.
  • Permission scopes you grant (e.g., video.upload, video.publish).
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens used to perform actions on your behalf.

We do not access your TikTok password, private messages, or any data you have not explicitly authorized.

1.3 Automatically collected information

  • Device information (model, operating system, app version).
  • Log data (IP address, access timestamps, error reports).
  • Usage analytics (features used, session duration).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain core App functionality.
  • To upload videos and content to your TikTok account at your request.
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account.
  • To communicate with you about service updates, security alerts, and support.
  • To improve App performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With TikTok: When you upload content, we transmit it to TikTok's servers via the official Content Posting API in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, and customer support, bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice to affected users.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and used only to perform actions you have authorized.

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with TikTok. Your use of TikTok features is also governed by TikTok's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of TikTok or any other third-party service.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Revoke TikTok authorization at any time via your TikTok account settings.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email where appropriate.
